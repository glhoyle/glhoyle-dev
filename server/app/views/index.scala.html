About Me

My name is Gracen Hoyle, and I am a junior at Trinity University in San Antonio, Texas. I am a computer science major and a classical studies minor, with an interest in game development and web applications.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing video games and creating digital and traditional art. I firmly believe that technical knowledge and creativity go hand in hand, and that computer science is an inherently artistic field focused on the creation of software and content. I'm also very interested in the field of virtual reality and the potential it holds, both for creating fiction and helping people explore the world around them.

Notable Experience

I have a few roles while I'm on campus. I work as a marketing intern in Trinity's Center for Experiental Learning and Career Success, helping to advertise events and seminars hosted by the office. This semester I am working at the campus newspaper, the Trinitonian, as a graphic designer and web assistant. In the past semester, I worked as a peer tutor for the Trinity chapter of the Association for Computer Machinery tutoring first-year computer science students.
